Tierra Whack

Tierra Whack is an American rapper who has received widespread acclaim for her quirky and theatrical take on contemporary R&B.

A brief timeline of Tierra Whack's career:

2017:

  • Whack signed with Interscope Records.
  • Whack made her debut as Tierra Whack with 3 tracks, including "Toe Jam" and "Mumbo Jumbo".

2018:

  • Whack released her debut album, Whack World.
  • Pitchfork endowed Whack with a "Best New Music" accolade, and she was praised for the atypical format of the album, with each track lasting about one minute.

2019-present:

  • In a series she called "#whackhistorymonth", Whack released one single per week for five weeks in February of 2019.
  • Whack collaborated with well-known artists Melanie Martinez and Flying Lotus.
  • The 2019 festival season featured Whack a number of times, at events including Coachella, Lollapalooza, and Austin City Limits.
